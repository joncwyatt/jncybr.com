Park Visit Log

Visit · Log · Remember

A personal tracker for the 63 US National Parks. Log every visit with photos and notes, watch your progress grow on a live map, earn milestone badges, and relive your park memories. Built to be fun and lightweight — no account required, no tracking, all data stays on your device.

Highlights

Features

  • ✓ Track visits to all 63 National Parks with photos and notes
  • ✓ Interactive map with filter overlays and GPS check-in
  • ✓ Progress dashboard with milestone achievements
  • ✓ Year in review, states progress, and landscape type tracking
  • ✓ Wishlist, shareable milestone cards, and surprise park picker
  • ✓ Live NPS data: alerts, hours, fees, and official photos
  • ✓ 100% on-device. No account, no server, no tracking.
