Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jon Wyatt ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Park Visit Log mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your experience is secure.

Section 1

Information We Collect

Anonymized Usage Data: To understand how our App is used and to identify popular destinations, we collect anonymized, aggregated data regarding which National Parks users are tracking or visiting within the App. This data is strictly statistical and is not linked to your identity, name, email address, or specific device.

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymized data we collect to analyze trends, see which parks are most popular among our user base, improve the App's features and user experience, and make informed decisions about future updates and content additions.

Section 3

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated park tracking statistics (which contain no personally identifiable information) with prospective buyers, investors, or the general public. We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Section 4

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as Apple's standard App Store analytics or independent crash reporting tools) that may collect information used to identify your device for troubleshooting purposes. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies.

Section 5

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of the anonymized data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Section 6

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

Section 7

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the Effective Date at the top of this page and, if the changes are significant, by providing a prominent notice within the App.
